1 System Overview

The Driver Schedule System is a web-based scheduling platform built for Magenta Home Health. It allows the admin team to plan and publish daily driver-nurse transport schedules, while drivers and nurses can log in to view their own schedules, contact each other via WhatsApp, and report late arrivals.

The system runs entirely in a web browser — no app installation is required.

2 User Roles

Admin

Full access to create and manage schedules, add/edit/delete drivers and nurses, view all late reports, and import users via CSV. There is one admin account per system.

Driver

Can view their own assigned schedules (today, calendar, all). Can report a nurse as late and send a WhatsApp message to any nurse on their schedule.

Nurse

Can view their own pickup schedule (today, calendar, all). Can send a WhatsApp message to their assigned driver to notify readiness.

3 First-Time Setup

When the system is accessed for the first time (before any admin account exists), it automatically redirects to the Setup page.

  1. Open the system URL in your browser. You will be taken to the Setup page automatically.
  2. Enter your Full Name, Email Address, and a secure Password (minimum 8 characters).
  3. Re-enter the password in the Confirm Password field.
  4. Click Create Admin Account.
  5. You will see a success message. Click Go to Login to proceed.
⚠️ This setup page only appears once. Once the admin account is created, the setup page is permanently disabled for security.
🔒 Choose a strong password. There is no self-service password reset — only the admin can change passwords for other users.
4 Logging In
  1. Open the system URL. Click Go to Login on the home page.
  2. Enter your Email Address and Password.
  3. Click Sign In.
  4. You will be redirected to your dashboard based on your role:
    Admins → Admin dashboard  |  Drivers & Nurses → My Schedule dashboard.
✅ Your session stays active for 8 hours. You will be taken back to the login page automatically when it expires.

Logging Out

Click the Logout button in the top-right corner of any page.

5 Admin Guide

5.1 Dashboard & Stats

The admin dashboard opens on the Today tab and shows five summary cards at the top:

  • Drivers — total number of driver accounts
  • Nurses — total number of nurse accounts
  • Total Schedules — all schedules ever created
  • Upcoming — schedules from today onwards
  • Late Reports (red) — late reports filed today; click to jump to the Late Reports tab

5.2 Managing Schedules

Creating a New Schedule

  1. Go to the All Schedules tab and click + Add Schedule, or go to the Today tab and click + Add for Today.
  2. Select the Date and assign a Driver from the dropdown.
  3. Click + Add Nurse to add a nurse trip. Fill in:
    • Nurse — select from dropdown
    • Pickup Time — time to pick up this nurse
    • Pickup Location — address where nurse will be picked up
    • Drop Location — destination (e.g. hospital name)
  4. Repeat + Add Nurse for each nurse on this schedule. Use the × Remove button to delete a nurse row.
  5. Add optional Notes for the driver.
  6. Click Save Schedule.

Editing a Schedule

Click the Edit button on any schedule row. The same form opens with existing data pre-filled. Make changes and click Save Schedule.

Deleting a Schedule

Click the Delete button on the schedule row and confirm the prompt.

📌 You can assign multiple nurses to a single driver on the same date — each nurse gets their own pickup time and locations.

5.3 Calendar View

Click the 📆 Calendar tab. The current month is displayed with coloured indicators:

  • Green dot — upcoming scheduled date
  • Grey dot — past scheduled date
  • Blue border — today's date

Click any date to see the schedules for that day below the calendar. Click + Add Schedule for this date to create one directly from the calendar.

Use the ← Prev and Next → buttons to navigate months.

5.4 Managing Drivers

  1. Go to the 🚗 Drivers tab.
  2. Click + Add Driver to create a new driver account. Fill in Name, Email, Phone, and Password.
  3. Click Edit on any row to update their details. Leave the password field blank to keep the existing password.
  4. Click Delete to remove a driver. Their past schedules remain but will show as Unassigned.

The 💬 WhatsApp icon next to a driver's phone opens a direct WhatsApp chat.

5.5 Managing Nurses

Works identically to the Drivers tab. Go to the 👩‍⚕️ Nurses tab to add, edit, or delete nurse accounts.

5.6 CSV Bulk Import

To add many drivers or nurses at once:

  1. Go to the Drivers or Nurses tab and click ⬆ Import CSV.
  2. Click ⬇ Download Sample CSV to get the correct template.
  3. Fill in the CSV with columns: name, email, password, phone (phone is optional).
  4. Click Select CSV File and choose your file. A preview table will appear.
  5. Click Import. A summary shows how many were imported and how many were skipped (duplicate emails are skipped automatically).
✅ You can import the same CSV again safely — duplicate emails are detected and skipped.

5.7 Late Reports

Go to the ⚠️ Late Reports tab to see all late arrival reports filed by drivers. Each report shows:

  • The date of the schedule
  • The nurse who was late
  • The driver who filed the report
  • The exact time the report was submitted

The Late Reports stat card on the dashboard shows how many reports were filed today.

6 Driver Guide

After logging in, drivers are taken to the My Schedule dashboard. Your profile (name, email, phone) is shown at the top.

6.1 Today's Schedule

The ☀️ Today tab shows all your nurse trips for today. Each trip card displays:

  • Nurse name and phone number
  • Pickup time
  • Pickup location → Drop location
  • WhatsApp button to message the nurse
  • ⏰ Report Late button

6.2 Reporting a Late Nurse

  1. Find the nurse's trip card in your Today tab.
  2. Click the ⏰ Report Late button on their card.
  3. Confirm the prompt. The button changes to "⏰ Late Reported".
📌 You can only report a nurse as late once per schedule. The report is visible to the admin immediately.

6.3 WhatsApp Contact

  1. On any nurse trip card, click the 💬 WhatsApp button.
  2. WhatsApp opens (web or app) with a pre-filled message: "Hi [Nurse Name], I've arrived at the pickup location."
  3. Review and send the message.
✅ Nurse phone numbers must be entered in the system by the admin for the WhatsApp button to appear.
7 Nurse Guide

After logging in, nurses are taken to the My Schedule dashboard showing their assigned trips.

7.1 Today's Schedule

The ☀️ Today tab shows your trip for today. Each card displays:

  • Date and pickup time (shown large)
  • Pickup location and drop location
  • Your assigned driver's name and phone
  • WhatsApp button to contact the driver

Use the 📅 Calendar and 📋 All Schedules tabs to view upcoming and past trips.

7.2 WhatsApp Contact

  1. On your schedule card, click 💬 WhatsApp Driver.
  2. WhatsApp opens with a pre-filled message: "Hi [Driver Name], I'm ready for pickup."
  3. Review and send the message.
8 Tips & FAQ

Why can't I see any schedule today?

The admin may not have created a schedule for today yet. Contact your admin to add one.

The WhatsApp button is not showing

The phone number for that driver or nurse has not been added in the system. Ask the admin to update the profile with a phone number.

I forgot my password

Contact your admin. The admin can update your password from the Drivers or Nurses management tab.

How do I view past schedules?

Go to the 📋 All Schedules tab or use the 📅 Calendar to navigate to previous months.

Phone number format

Enter Australian phone numbers starting with 04 (e.g. 0412 345 678). The system converts them automatically to international format for WhatsApp.

Session expired — why was I logged out?

Sessions last 8 hours. If you were inactive for a long period, you will be redirected to the login page. Simply log in again.

Can multiple nurses be assigned to one driver?

Yes. When creating a schedule, use + Add Nurse to add as many nurse trips as needed under one driver.
